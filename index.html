Our mission

Make every Australian cattle farm carbon-intelligent and carbon-profitable — measuring to recognised standards, predicting hotspots, prescribing action, and converting reductions to ACCU value.

The gap we fill

No single platform connected operational data, LCA modelling, AI forecasting and ACCU monetisation for cattle farms. LCCIP unifies the full decision cycle.

Why it matters

Cattle farming is one of Australia's largest emission sources. Farm-level intelligence unlocks Scope 3 supply-chain trust, net-zero progress and new farm income.
