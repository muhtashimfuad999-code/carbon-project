Calculate your farm's carbon footprint

Quick estimate for a fast answer, or a full property assessment structured like the national standard. Emissions are computed by a swappable engine — today NGER/IPCC-aligned factors, designed to run on the AIA Environmental Accounting Platform engine once connected.

Engine: LCCIP indicative (NGER/IPCC-aligned) — EAP-ready Structured to the national standard · property → enterprises → inventory → results

Property

Define the property boundary and land use — the basis of the assessment.

Enterprises & livestock

Add each enterprise on the property. Livestock are entered by class — the biggest driver of farm emissions.

Feed, fertiliser & soil

Purchased inputs carry embedded (Scope 3) emissions; nitrogen drives N₂O.

Energy, fuel & transport

Scope 1 (fuel you burn) and Scope 2 (electricity you buy).

Production & credit scenario

Production gives carbon intensity. The reduction scenario drives the ACCU calculation (baseline − project).

Methods & standards

How our carbon is calculated — and how it becomes ACCUs

Transparent, NGER / IPCC-aligned, and built to plug into the real Australian carbon-credit scheme. This page is what we show to auditors, processors and the Clean Energy Regulator.

Mechanism

The carbon emission mechanism

Total emissions = Σ ( activity × emission factor )

Three gases → one unit (CO₂-e)

CO₂ ×1  — diesel, electricity
CH₄ ×28 — cattle & manure
N₂O ×265 — fertiliser & manure

Methane dominates cattle carbon — a little counts as a lot of CO₂-e.

Cradle-to-farm-gate LCA · Scopes 1·2·3

We count on-farm and embedded supply-chain emissions, then express them per unit of product (kg CO₂-e / L milk or / kg beef). Sorted into Scope 1 (direct), Scope 2 (energy) and Scope 3 (feed & transport) — the standard used by processors and lenders.

Why it's more than a calculator

Normal calculator vs LCCIP (LCA)

A normal footprint calculator

  • On-farm emissions only — stops at the fence
  • Ignores embedded feed & fertiliser emissions
  • Gives one lump total
  • No land / vegetation carbon
  • No credit pathway

LCCIP — life-cycle assessment

  • Cradle-to-farm-gate boundary (stated)
  • Counts embedded Scope 3 (feed, transport)
  • Reports intensity per litre / per kg
  • Includes land sequestration & land-use change
  • Shapes reductions into ACCUs (baseline − project)
Honest status. LCCIP produces audit-ready, NGER/IPCC-aligned data to support carbon projects. It does not itself issue ACCUs — certification requires an approved CER method, baseline and independent verification.
